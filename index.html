2014
Ian Goodfellow codes the first generative adversarial network (GAN) in 2014. The GAN is trained to generate images of handwritten digits but shows promising results in other domains, such as faces.
2018
After years of improvement, NVIDIA releases StyleGAN, the first time generated faces look deceptively realistic.
May, 2023
Anyone can create realistic images within a few clicks. This generated image of an explosion at the pentagon crashed the stock market, taking off half a trillion dollars in market cap from the S&P 500.
June, 2023
Midjourney releases the 5.2 version of its model, which specializes in generating photorealistic images from a text prompt.
Today
The open source community has created a number of tools to circumvent content moderation by large text-to-image providers. The possibilities are endless.
